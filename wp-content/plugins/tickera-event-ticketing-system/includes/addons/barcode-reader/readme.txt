== Description ==

Barcode readers improve the whole check-in process, decrease the time of the check-in, leave less crowd at the door of your venue and make your attendees more happy! Barcode reader add-on for Tickera works nicely with your Staff role. So, you'll be able to allow your staff / check-in team to check in attendees in an easy and fast way!
